# Background Music Feature - Quick Start Guide

## For Users: How to Add Background Music to Your Bio

### Step 1: Get a Music URL
You need a direct link to an audio file (MP3, OGG, or WAV format):
- Upload your music to a hosting service (e.g., Firebase Storage, AWS S3, etc.)
- Get the direct download link
- Example: `https://example.com/my-music.mp3`

### Step 2: Add to Dashboard
1. Go to Dashboard
2. Click on **Profile** tab
3. Scroll to **Background Music** section
4. Paste your music URL into the input field
5. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Preview
Your phone preview will immediately show the music player below your bio description.

### Step 4: Share
Your public bio page will now display:
- Your business name
- Bio description
- üéµ **Music player** with controls
- Social links and other content below

## Tips & Best Practices

### Audio File Selection
- **File Size**: Keep under 10MB for quick loading
- **Duration**: 30 seconds to 5 minutes works best for looping
- **Format**: MP3 is most compatible across all browsers
- **Quality**: 128kbps MP3 is good balance of quality vs. file size

### Musical Style
- **Ambient**: Soft, non-intrusive background (best choice)
- **Jazz/Lo-fi**: Relaxing and professional
- **Upbeat**: Use sparingly, may distract visitors
- **Brand Music**: Use if it matches your brand identity

### Example Good URLs
```
https://storage.googleapis.com/mybucket/ambient-bg-music.mp3
https://cdn.example.com/music/lofi-beats.mp3
```

## Troubleshooting

### Music Player Doesn't Show
- Check that you've entered a valid music URL
- Save your profile again
- Refresh the page

### Music Won't Play
- Browser may have blocked auto-play
- Click play button to start manually
- Try a different audio format
- Check browser volume isn't muted

### Player Looks Wrong
- Clear browser cache and refresh
- Try a different browser
- Update your browser to latest version

### Slow Loading
- Use a smaller audio file
- Use MP3 format (best compression)
- Check your hosting service

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change the music later?**
A: Yes! Just update the URL in your Dashboard and save.

**Q: Will the music slow down my page?**
A: No, music is streamed on demand. Small impact on page load.

**Q: Can visitors download the music?**
A: Depends on your hosting. The player doesn't offer download.

**Q: Does music play on mobile?**
A: Yes, but may require tap to play due to browser policies.

**Q: Can I use Spotify music?**
A: Not directly. Need a direct MP3 link, not Spotify URL.

**Q: How long can the music be?**
A: Any length, but 2-5 minutes works best for looping.

**Q: Will it work with YouTube music?**
A: Not directly. Need a direct audio file link.

**Q: Can I have multiple songs?**
A: Currently one background music. Multiple playlists coming soon!
